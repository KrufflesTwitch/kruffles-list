TwitchToolkit

TwitchToolkit is a mod written by Twitch.tv/hodlhodl that integrates storytelling decisions into chat votes, awards viewers coins for watching, and those coins can be spent on items/events in game.

  • !bal - check your coin balance
  • !buy billiardstable - buy a billiardstable (Example, choose anything from the list!)
  • !buy beer 6 - buy 6 beers (Example, choose anything from the list!)
  • !buy party - buy a party for the colony (Example, choose anything from the list!)
  • !buy ticket - jointhe name queue and enter for chance to win a colonist
  • !buy pawn - buy a pawn for yourself
  • !gambleskill medical 1500 - spend 1500 points on your medical skill
  • !mypawnskills - check your pawns skills
  • !mypawnstory - learn more about your pawn
  • !buy pawn - buy a pawn for yourself
  • !buy addtrait - craft the ultimate colonist
  • !buy removetrait - taketh away
  • !lookup item medicine - searches item databased for "medicine"
  • !lookup event raid Lookup for Events "raid": Raid, Dropraid, Sapperraid, Siegeraid, Mechanoidraid
  • !giftcoins - Syntax: !giftcoins @username 100 - give another viewer your coins
  • IMPORTANT - Raids and Diseases listed below have changed! Ask in chat or use !lookup
